HOLINESS

Silence makes the pin fall.

Absence touches the piano.

The shadow sleeps in shoes.

In the backyard
  leaps the heart of a bird.

It's the time of mirrors
  exuding through the hands.

It's the irony
  of dead man's peace;
    when the teeth of God
      have already devoured you…

What time can it be
  in the castaway's clock?

Search for the girl
  who makes you pray in sin;
    she has the pitcher
      where she stirs up lightning.

In chastity
  there are only naked virgins
    staring at the birds.

We are almost, almost,
  almost holy!
