RAINBOW

The Rainbow
  is a boy who paints
  the blouses of peasants
  and brightens smiles
  with his legs, pink lusts,
  and other herbs.

The lovely outfit
  spans the horizon
  over the sown fields
  and awakens his colors.

God rejoices
  when crossing the magic boards,
  then, with humility, ignites
  the stars…

And when the wine cellars are extinguished,
  later, much later, in the evening
  the rainbow enters singing softly
  in a poem and disappears:

    The boy lost his golden thread.
