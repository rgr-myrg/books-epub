THE VOICE AND THE MIRRORS

God is a gray substance in the mirror,
  almost a word,
    sometimes, nothing.

Mirrors are empty-filled with faces
  of the dead
    mimicking gestures
      and the needles of dreams.

The firefly is extinguished and resurrected,
  the twilight has yellow voices,
  innocence envelops fear with its light,
  we travel in a wide-open casket,
  the voice jumps out the window,
  and only leaves stains behind,
  words dash, naked, over the stones,
  silence cracks its head open,
  the void is filled with darkness,
  the midpoint is a magical mirage,
  false truths befuddle those who seek
  the light on their footsteps,
  conjectures build arches,
  fanaticism creates saints
  and the sea, the sea, the sea
  hits our core, from side to sideâ€¦

Until blood runs over the glass.

Music
  is a water lily
    over the river.

This is how poems fall
  with tenderness
    like dry leaves in the wind
      nonetheless, in the depths
        of the mirror,
          fireflies overflow…

When the mirrors are broken
  every fragment
    is a voice in the wind!
